About DriveSafe

DriveSafe is your personal driving assistant. Using smart sensors and AI, it tracks your driving habits, detects risky behavior, and gives personalized feedback — helping you become a safer driver every day.

Ideal for everyday drivers, parents, and fleet managers who value safety and awareness.
